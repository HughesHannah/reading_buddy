Reading Buddy is a collaborative website where users can record and review books that they have read. Users can read comments from other users on books and add them to their ‘Wishlist’. Once they have read the book, they can then add it to their ‘Read’ list and write a review for future readers. A top trending books section shows which books are currently most popular.
